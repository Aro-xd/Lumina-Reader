# 📚 Lumina Reader

A sleek, modern, and lightweight **EPUB reader** built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Lumina Reader allows you to enjoy your favorite books directly in your browser.

https://aro-xd.github.io/Lumina-Reader/

---

## ✨ Features

*   **Privacy-Focused**: Books are processed locally in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server.
*   **Reading Progress**: Real-time tracking of your reading progress in percentages.
*   **Responsive Navigation**: Easy-to-use controls and keyboard support (Arrow keys).

---

## 🚀 How to use

1.  **Open** the application in any web browser.
2.  **Drag and Drop** your favorite `.epub` file into the upload zone or click to browse.
3.  **Start Reading!** Use the "Next" and "Previous" buttons or your keyboard's arrow keys to navigate.
